About Metis

Metis is an Ethereum Layer 2 optimistic rollup platform originally forked from Optimism and launched on mainnet on 19 November 2021. It is an easy-to-use, highly scalable, low-cost, and fully functional Layer 2 framework that supports a wide range of use cases, including yield farming, DEX trading, and powering the gig economy via dApps that offer cheap and fast micropayments.
