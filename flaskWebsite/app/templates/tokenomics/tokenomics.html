Tokenomics

Phase Ⅰ:

During the 1st phase, the ICO will be active as well as an active burn campaign. Tweet the hashtag 05980134 and it will count towards burning up to 10% of the total supply. More tweets, more tokens burned.

Phase Ⅱ:

Immediatly after the ICO, another burn will begin and last 6 months. The community will get to vote on hashtags of their choosing to count towards burning. As they tweet the hashtag, that will count towards burning more tokens

Phase Ⅲ:

After Phase II, a staking period will begin and work similarly as the burn worked. However, instead of burning tokens, the community will instead use the same voting process to earn a percent for the tokens they have staked.

Automatic Liquidity Pool

Half of the Metis used in the ICO will be sent along with the corresponding amount of EFTT to create a Liquidity Pool on Netswap. The liquidity will remain locked for 6 months after ICO end. What EFTT isn't used to create liquidity will be burned.
